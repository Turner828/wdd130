Overview

Purpose

You open the doors, shaking with excitement for the midnight release of the final movie to the series you have been following for years. You smell the fresh scent of popcorn, candy, and nachos. You hear laughing and arcade games popping off in the background. You go to the booth and get yourself a soda pop and a popcorn ready to witness this masterpiece you have been waiting months for. You turn in your ticket, find a perfect spot, and are ready to watch this movie. You look up and you see your room’s ceiling. You hear your annoying alarm yelling in your ear for you to get up! You dreamt it. You were not there, but you cannot stop thinking about getting there to watch this cinematic masterpiece but can-not decide where to go see it. No issue here with Movie Marathon where anyone can go online to our website and buy your tickets and seats anywhere. Remove the stress of hoping there’s tickets left and long lines. Move Marathon has you covered with everything one can need to jump into their world of movies.

Audience

My directed audience is for those who love to experience cinema and want a gentle breeze of an experience. Too often going to the movies can be hectic trying to get a ticket for everyone and grabbing popcorn and candy for every intime for the movie. With Movie Marathon all that can be taken care of for you and organized into a smooth and easy way of understanding.
